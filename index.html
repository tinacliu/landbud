What is House Genie ?

A new approach to property management for the self-manage landlord. House Genie becomes your personal assistant in dealing with all tenants enquires, helping to answer common questions and solve maintanance issues without the need to bothering you.

House Genie is the FIRST smart web software to learn dynamically about your properties. Your property profile is tailor-made on day one and constly self-improving in an effort to evetually handle all tenants enquires.
