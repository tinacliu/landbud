What is House Genie ?

A new approach to property management for the self-managed landlord. House Genie becomes your personal assistant in dealing with all tenants enquires, helping to answer common questions and solve maintenance issues without the need to bother you.

House Genie is the FIRST smart web software to learn dynamically about your properties. Your property profile is tailor-made on day one and constantly self-improving to eventually handle almost all tenants enquires.

created purely for the self-managed landlords

What we do for you

screen all enquires

so non-urgent questions will be queued and only hit your inbox Mon - Fri 9 am to 5 pm.

reactive repair reporting

after being taken through steps in specify exactly where and what the issue is, tenants are offered tailored solutions

house manual creation

we create a house manual tailored to your property and pull information like rubbish collection dates from your local council

optional 24/7 urgent call out

verified urgent issues can be directed to our 24/7 emergency call-out service while keeping you informed

documents management

easy to keep track of key tenancy related information and get reminded about documents expiry dates

Getting started is easy

  1. Sign up with House Genie

    Takes 2 mins to sign up, tell us basic informaiton about you and your property.

  2. Our property specialist attends your property

    We will phone you to arrange a visit to your property where our property specialist will note down information needed in creating your tailored house manual and repair guidance system.

  3. We create your smart property profile

    Your property will have a tailored house manual, a repairs reporting and solving system, and a FAQ section. We constantly improve this system to reduce enquiries going to you.

  4. Your hassle-free landlord life starts!

    We send your tenants a welcome email explaining we are now your online property manager, and how they should use the system from now on.
